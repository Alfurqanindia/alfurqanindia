Our Projects

AL FURQAN HIFZUL QURAN COLLEGE
This institution for Hifzul Quran (Memorisation of holly Quran) is one of the institutional diversities of Al furqan. Students who have passed 4th standard are selected to this institution for course duration of 3 years. They can continue the school education after its successful completion. With the grace of God, the almighty, the institution has succeeded in bringing out a number of hafiz scholars to the community.
AL FURQAN KIDS GARDEN
Al furqan kids garden is constituted with an aim of helping future generation mould their lives according to Islam ic values enhanced through child friendly mentoring. This will be done using the modern methods of education with Islamic studies. Every modern, state of the art technologies will be used effectively in the class rooms.
