About us

Al furqan

Al furqan islamic da'awa Centre is a multi faceted education centre which is being established at Teakkum tottam, Poonoor, Calicut.kerala The aim of Al Furqan is to form a new generation which is dedicated to keeping justice, serving mankind and making them volunteers of the society. It includes a programme for charity also.

Our Services

Women quran study centre

we conduct quran class for womens and give them the messege of islam and quran which will make them rich by culture.

Hajj Umra classes

al furqan conducts hajj class and umra class by which people will get knowledge about hajj and umra,espessially who are going for hajj and umra.

Adopting orphans

we adopt orphans and ppoor students and lookafter their study and expenses.The activity prophet(s) most love.

Heath care and relief

al furqan owns a van which use for taking patients to hospital(madical care centre) and bringing them back home. And we supply food kit for poor in arabic month ramzan and in other ocassions.

Medical awareness and camps

conducts camp for making people aware about their health and free checkup

Career guidence classes

Youth are future,al furqan conducts career guidence class when the public exams finish(1oth and +2) through which our students can choose their choices and clear their dougbts.
